Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I change the unlock date?

No, once a capsule is created, the unlock date cannot be changed. This ensures the integrity of your time capsule.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use military-grade encryption to protect all your data. Your memories are safe with us.

How do I add friends?

Go to your profile and click on "Add Friend". Enter their username and send a friend request.

My account was deleted. Can I get it back?

If your account was deleted by mistake, please use the "Request Account Restore" link in the footer and provide the details. Our admins will review your request.

How do I contact support?

You can use this contact form or email us directly. We typically respond within 24 hours.
